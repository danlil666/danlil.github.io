This last one is made with a bit of my own adaptation to the original model. As a continuation of my previous project that uses the PCB board as part of the look, I made the PCB board in this small lamp Bing Dwen Dwen’s ski board. This is a gift for a friend. I wanted to use my old trick that makes the board touch activated, but I’m cheap. Then I throw in a slide switch.

Getting the battery and the charger into the smallest possible space is the challenge. But otherwise this project is pretty straight forward.
